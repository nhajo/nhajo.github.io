Creme brulee

Crème brûlée is a classic French dessert, consisting of a pudding topped with burnt brown sugar; resulting in a delightful crispy top with a soft pudding base.

Figure 8: Crème brûlée served with finished burnt sugar topping.

Ingredients

The following list of ingredients serves 4 people.

  • 0.7 vanilla bean or some drops of vanilla extract
  • 3.3 dl cream
  • 0.7 dl milk
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 1 dl sugar

Topping:

  • 0.7 dl brown sugar or raw sugar

Instructions

  1. Split the vanilla bean and put it in a pot with cream and milk. Heat until lukewarm.
  2. Whip yolks and sugar, then add to the cream mixture. Let rest overnight in fridge.
  3. Set oven to 100°C.
  4. Pour batter into oven-safe dishes and bake for 45–50 minutes.
  5. Cool in fridge for a few hours.
  6. Top with sugar and burn it with a torch.
