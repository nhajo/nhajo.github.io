Meiling porridge

This soy-based rice porridge is little-known outside China but worth trying.

Ingredients

For 1 serving:

  • 0.06 dl regular rice
  • 0.26 dl glutinous rice
  • 2 dl soy milk
  • Small piece of sweet potato or yam
  • 2 tbsp sugar or rock sugar
Instructions
  1. Soak rice overnight.
  2. Boil soy milk and rice.
  3. Simmer 15 minutes.
  4. Chop sweet potato/yam.
  5. Steam or boil, mash with fork.
  6. Add mash to porridge.
  7. Simmer 20 more minutes to desired thickness.
  8. Add sugar. Adjust soy milk if needed.
