Kladdkaka

Kladdkaka, or Sticky Cake, is a simple Swedish chocolate cake. It’s known for its ease and charm. Optional ingredients add personal flair.

Ingredients

  • 200g butter
  • 3 dl sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla sugar or extract
  • 1 dl cocoa
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 dl flour
  • Optional: 1 tbsp coffee liquor
  • Optional: ½ cup brewed coffee or 3 tbsp instant
  • Optional: 2 dl pine nuts

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C.
  2. Roast pine nuts.
  3. Melt butter, cool slightly.
  4. Mix in sugar, cocoa, vanilla, optional coffee/liquor.
  5. Add eggs one at a time.
  6. Pour into buttered, crumb-coated pan (~24cm).
  7. Add pine nuts (optional).
  8. Bake 10–15 minutes.
  9. Serve with ice cream or whipped cream.
